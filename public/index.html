Meet IdentifEye

IdentifEye is a micro-lending platform for stacked online courses. We aim to empower students to build their skillset as demanded by the industry and we help industries better allocate their social good funds

Queue up to experience IdentifEye!

IdentifEye connects all

IdentifEye brings Students, MOOC platforms, Tech Companies and Banks on one platform to help each other

For Students

Students live on budget, enrolling for MOOCs and Nano-Degrees can sometimes be expensive, IdentifEye provides quick low interest micro-loans to such students as quickly as it takes to click a button

For MOOC platforms

MOOC platforms can use our platform to advertise their courses and add a "PAY WITH IDENTIFEYE" button on their websites, our efficient APIs will take care of the rest, and will direct applicants to a seamless payment experience

For Tech Companies

We channel funds by obtaining funding from tech companies willing to disburse funds for their social good programs, in turn channelising students towards enrolling in specific courses equipping them with skills required by the tech industry, Helping build a wider talent pool

For Banks

Banks can tie up with us and provide quick loans through our platform, we help them by facilitating customer acquisition by engaging students early in their university period

How does it work?

A sneak peek into the magic we do behind the screens

Machine Learning Based Credit Risk Assessment

Our machine learning models devise a quick and a reliable credit score by considering multiple data points such as academic transcripts, university ID, social media credentials etc

Quick Loan Disbursement

Based on the credit-score, a small fee is calculated that the user has to pay, and then the loan amount is granted with a repayment window of 3 months to 1.5 years

Easy to use Mobile Application

A user centric application with an easy to use UI helps students browse through a spectrum of online courses and enroll in them all at one place. The app helps users easily manage their loan payment schedule and order lists. With state of the art UI, we aim to provide an enriching experience to every user
